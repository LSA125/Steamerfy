About Steamify

Steamify is a quiz app that asks you questions based on your Steam library. Enter your Steam ID, and we'll generate a quiz tailored to the games you own. Test your own knowledge or challenge your friends and see who comes out on top!

How to Find Your Steam ID

To find your Steam ID, follow these simple steps:

  • 1. Open Steam and log in to your account.
  • 2. Click on your profile name in the top-right corner and select 'View Account Details'.
  • 3. Your Steam ID is located just under the "X's Account" at the top of the page.

How to Steamify Works

Steamify queries the free Steam API, grabs the games in each player's Steam library along with how many hours they have played it, and some metadata like images and names. It randomly generates a question based on the player's Steam library. If you find a bug, feel free to contact me at lsaweb277@gmail.com.
